## 🛠️ Features

- ✅ Get a list of official Indian public holidays
- ✅ Format dates in Indian-friendly formats (DD-MM-YYYY, etc.)
- ✅ Ideal for:
  - School/College Attendance Systems
  - HR & Leave Management
  - Calendar Apps
